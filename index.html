Frequently asked questions

Before your home visit

Which areas of Bangalore do you serve?

Availability depends on your location and appointment schedule. Share your area or PIN code on WhatsApp so the team can confirm service coverage.

Can every dental treatment be done at home?

No. Some assessments and selected services may be suitable for home care, while treatments needing advanced equipment or imaging should be completed in a dental clinic. We will guide you appropriately.

What should I arrange before the dentist arrives?

A clean, well-lit space with comfortable access is usually helpful. Any specific requirements will be shared while confirming your appointment.

Can I book for an elderly parent or family member?

Yes. You can enquire on behalf of a family member. Please share their age, main dental concern, general location, and any mobility considerations.

Is home dental care suitable for an emergency?

Severe swelling, uncontrolled bleeding, facial trauma, breathing difficulty, or other urgent symptoms may need immediate emergency care. Do not wait for a routine home appointment.
